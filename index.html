Welcome to Los Tacos Guapos! We are proud to announce we are open for business. We are a Mexican style taqueria that is currently only working through a food truck. We plan to expand and open multiple stores to sell our products. We sell an assortment of Mexican foods such as tacos, quesadillas, tortas, and sopes. We hope to make Mexican bakery confections in the future.

 

If you have come to find delicious, handsome tacos you've come to the right place! We are a new business that has started Spring of 2023 and we are located in Forest City, NC.
